# Demo Script

## Demo 3 phut cho hackathon/PM

Muc tieu: cho nguoi xem thay flow that va diem khac biet cua CXV trong thoi gian ngan.

1. Mo `Dashboard`.
   - Noi: "Day la command center cua InnovateX Smart Port. Thay vi dieu phoi bi dong, he thong hien slot xanh, muc un tac, CO2 saved va diem xanh theo thoi gian gan that."

2. Vao `Booking`.
   - Chon xe, cang, khung gio mong muon va muc tieu `ECO` hoac `LOW_CONGESTION`.
   - Bam `Chay GreenSlot AI`.
   - Noi: "Tai xe khong can tu doan gio nao vang. AI so sanh slot theo suc chua, thoi gian cho, un tac, diem xanh va CO2."

3. Giai thich recommendation card.
   - Chi vao wait minutes, utilization, green points, CO2 saved va ly do de xuat.
   - Noi: "Khac biet cua san pham khong phai form dat lich, ma la goi y co giai thich va co tac dong van hanh."

4. Xac nhan dat lich.
   - Noi: "Booking duoc tao bang transaction de giu slot va khong vuot capacity."

5. Vao `Appointments`.
   - Admin/operator cap nhat status thanh `COMPLETED`.
   - Noi: "Khi completed, he thong cap tin chi xanh mot lan. Roadmap Execution la QR/Green Tally, camera quet bien so/container va cong mo muc tieu 30 giay."

6. Vao `Green Credits` va `Rewards`.
   - Noi: "Rewarding bien hanh vi dung gio, chon slot xanh thanh loi ich that: uu tien cong, giam phi, voucher."

7. Ket o `Reports`.
   - Noi: "Bao cao khong chi dem booking. No chung minh 5 tac dong: giam thu cong, giam cho, giam CO2, tang khung gio xanh va tiet kiem chi phi."

## Sales demo 5 phut cho khach hang/PM

Muc tieu: ban cau chuyen gia tri, khong chi demo tinh nang.

1. Problem framing.
   - "Cang/kho bai thuong bi un tac do xe den don vao gio cao diem. Tai xe cho lau, ton nhien lieu, phat thai CO2/NOx va dieu phoi thu cong kho minh bach."

2. Product positioning.
   - "InnovateX Smart Port la he thong dat lich vao cang thong minh theo concept CXV: Planning bang AI, Execution bang Green Tally, Rewarding bang tin chi xanh."

3. Dashboard value.
   - "Operation manager thay ngay tong lich, thoi gian cho trung binh, muc un tac slot, CO2 saved va cac slot xanh nen dieu phoi."

4. Booking value.
   - "Tai xe hoac dispatcher chon xe/cang/thoi gian, he thong de xuat slot tot nhat kem ly do. Dieu nay giam phu thuoc vao kinh nghiem ca nhan va giam xe den sai thoi diem."

5. Appointment/Execution value.
   - "Lich hen la nen cho check-in that: QR, camera bien so/container, Green Tally va cong mo nhanh. MVP hien tai da co lifecycle status va audit log."

6. Rewarding value.
   - "Diem xanh tao dong luc de tai xe chon gio thap diem va di dung lich. Uu dai co the la uu tien cong, giam phi dich vu hoac voucher logistics."

7. Reports/ROI value.
   - "PM/client thay duoc tac dong: giam cong viec thu cong, giam thoi gian cho, giam CO2, ti le slot xanh va uoc tinh chi phi tiet kiem."

8. Expansion roadmap.
   - "Sau MVP co the mo rong Company multi-tenant, invite users, export report, notification, QR check-in, camera integration, traffic city data va modal shift sang sa lan."

9. Closing.
   - "Gia tri ban dau la website dat lich cang thong minh co the ban/bangiao, giup logistics/cang giam cho, giam un tac va co du lieu van hanh xanh ro rang."
