# Slide Reference: CXV Cang Xanh Vietnam

Nguon: slide thuyet trinh hackathon cua founder.

Muc dich: giu lai y tuong goc de rebuild web khong bi lech concept.

## Thong diep lon

CXV chuyen dich mo hinh van hanh cang tu "bi dong" sang "chu dong".

## Van de goc

- Xe container cho 2-3 gio tai cong, lang phi nhien lieu va tang chi phi logistics.
- Quy trinh kiem soat xe, giay to va tallying con thu cong, cham va de sai.
- Xe no may cho tao CO2/NOx, anh huong khu dan cu.
- Thieu minh bach logistics co the tao khung hoang rac thai/container bo roi.

## Giai phap goc

- AI dat hen chu dong.
- AI de xuat "Khung gio Xanh" dua tren lich tau, tinh trang bai, giao thong.
- Dashboard dieu do cho nhan vien dieu do/logistics dispatcher.
- Web-app tai xe co gio hen, cong vao va QR code.
- Tin chi xanh da tang, tinh sau khi hoan thanh quy trinh.
- Green Tally: tai xe quet QR, camera quet bien so va so container, cong mo tu dong.
- Tich hop du lieu giao thong thanh pho de tranh tuyen duong khong the di/can tranh.
- Tu van modal shift: so sanh xe tai va sa lan ve thoi gian, chi phi, phat thai CO2.

## Quy trinh van hanh trong slide

Planning:

- Nhan vien dieu do dang nhap dashboard CXV.
- AI tu van, so sanh va de xuat.
- AI dat hen de xuat khung gio xanh toi uu.

Execution:

- Tai xe den cang dung gio.
- Tai xe quet QR tai cong.
- Camera quet bien so xe va so container.
- Cong tu dong mo trong 30 giay, khong can giay to.

Rewarding:

- Sau khi xe ra khoi cang, he thong tinh va thuong diem.
- AI check dung gio, xe dien, tau dung dien bo.
- Tin chi xanh duoc them vao tai khoan.

## 4 nhom user trong ke hoach trien khai

- Logistics Dispatcher.
- Truck Driver.
- Port Operator.
- Shipping Lines.

## Lo trinh tu slide

- Tap trung vao Dat hen Xanh va Tin chi Xanh.
- Mo rong San Tai che.
- Tich hop Van tai Da phuong thuc.

## 5 chi so tac dong nen dua vao dashboard/report

- Giam cong viec thu cong: tu 13 phut xuong 30 giay, tu dong 100%.
- Giam chi phi van tai: muc tieu < 29.000d/luot theo slide.
- Cai thien toc do giao hang: tu 2-3 gio cho xuong < 30 phut.
- Ket noi cang - thanh pho: giam phu thuoc duong bo, tu van sa lan.
- Thuc day ben vung: giam phat thai tai cang, khuyen khich cold ironing.

## Dieu can dua vao san pham v2

- Dung ngon ngu "chu dong" va "khung gio xanh" trong UI.
- Dashboard nen co section Planning, Execution, Rewarding.
- Booking/recommendation nen co ly do de xuat va canh bao ket xe.
- Appointment nen co QR/check-in concept trong cac module sau.
- Reports nen co 5 chi so tac dong, khong chi co so booking.
- Roadmap co modal shift/sa lan va integration giao thong thanh pho.
