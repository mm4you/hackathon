# PM Selling Points

Muc tieu: khi PM/project manager xem san pham, ho phai thay ro day khong chi la web demo, ma la mot san pham co van de that, gia tri kinh doanh ro va co kha nang trien khai.

## Thong diep chinh

InnovateX Smart Port giup cang, kho bai va doi xe giam un tac bang cach dat lich thong minh, phan bo xe vao khung gio toi uu, giam thoi gian cho va tao co che diem xanh de tai xe co dong luc tuan thu lich.

Thong diep theo slide CXV: chuyen dich van hanh cang tu "bi dong" sang "chu dong" bang AI dat hen, Green Tally va tin chi xanh.

## Van de san pham giai quyet

- Cang/kho bai bi un tac vao gio cao diem.
- Tai xe/container phai cho lau, ton nhien lieu va thoi gian.
- Dieu phoi thu cong kho nhin tong the va kho toi uu slot.
- Khong co co che khuyen khich tai xe chon gio thap diem hoac di dung lich.
- Quan ly kho theo doi tac dong van hanh va moi truong con thu cong.

## Nguoi dung chinh

- Admin/operation manager: theo doi luong xe, slot, bao cao va trang thai lich hen.
- Driver: dat lich vao cang/kho, xem goi y khung gio tot, theo doi diem xanh.
- Chu doi xe/cong ty logistics: co the mo rong sau de quan ly nhieu xe va bao cao hieu qua.

## Diem hay can the hien trong web

- AI Scheduling Engine goi y khung gio, khong bat tai xe tu doan.
- Goi y co ly do ro: thoi gian cho, muc un tac, suc chua con lai, diem xanh, CO2 tiet kiem.
- Co 3 muc tieu toi uu: vao nhanh, tranh un tac, toi da diem xanh/CO2.
- Admin dashboard cho thay tinh hinh van hanh ngay lap tuc.
- Green credits bien hanh vi tot thanh dong luc ro rang.
- Reports cho PM thay tac dong: booking, completed, wait time, CO2 saved, green points.
- Co QR/Green Tally concept cho tai xe check-in tai cong va camera quet bien so trong roadmap.
- Co modal shift concept: so sanh xe tai voi sa lan ve thoi gian, chi phi va CO2.

## Metrics PM nen thay

- So lich hen hom nay.
- Thoi gian cho trung binh.
- Ti le slot thap/trung/cao diem.
- CO2 saved.
- Green points issued.
- Top drivers.
- Lich hen theo status.
- Thoi gian xu ly cong: tu thu cong sang muc tieu 30 giay.
- Chi phi van tai uoc tinh tiet kiem.
- Ty le slot xanh/canh bao ket xe.

## Demo script ngan

1. Login bang tai khoan driver.
2. Vao booking, chon xe, cang, thoi gian va muc tieu toi uu.
3. Chay AI recommendation.
4. Giai thich recommendation card: vi sao slot nay tot.
5. Xac nhan dat lich.
6. Vao appointments xem lich moi.
7. Login/admin view, cap nhat lich thanh completed.
8. Quay lai driver xem diem xanh/reward/report.

## Cau chuyen ban cho PM

- San pham co problem-solution fit ro trong logistics/cang.
- Flow MVP ngan nhung cham dung loi dau van hanh.
- Khac biet nam o recommendation co giai thich, khong phai form dat lich binh thuong.
- Khac biet nam o cau chuyen "chu dong": AI de xuat truoc, tai xe check-in bang QR, he thong tu tinh tin chi xanh.
- Co the ban/bangiao nhu mot web hoan chinh cho doanh nghiep hoac dung lam nen tang phat trien tiep.

## Dieu can tranh

- UI dep nhung khong giai thich duoc gia tri.
- Dashboard nhieu so nhung so lieu khong lien quan den van hanh.
- AI recommendation noi chung chung, khong co ly do.
- Flow qua dai lam PM khong thay nhanh diem hay.
- Tinh nang qua nhieu nhung module loi.
